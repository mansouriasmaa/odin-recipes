Poultry Pastilla Recipe

Description

Poultry Pastilla is a traditional Moroccan dish that blends savory and sweet flavors in a flaky, golden pastry. Filled with spiced chicken, almonds, and eggs, it’s seasoned with saffron, cinnamon, and ginger, then topped with powdered sugar and cinnamon. This elegant pie is a highlight of Moroccan cuisine, often served at weddings and festive gatherings.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Filling:

  • 500g chicken breast or thighs, boneless and skinless
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon saffron threads
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup toasted almonds, chopped
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 package of warqa dough or phyllo dough

For the Pastilla Assembly:

  • 3-4 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps

  1. Prepare the Chicken Filling:
    • In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add chopped onions and garlic, cooking until softened.
    • Add the chicken, ginger, turmeric, cinnamon, saffron, salt, and pepper. Stir to coat the chicken with spices.
    • Add enough water to cover the chicken and let it simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the chicken is fully cooked and tender.
    • Once the chicken is done, remove it from the pot, shred it into small pieces, and set it aside.
    • In the same pot, reduce the sauce until thickened. Add the shredded chicken back in and stir in the chopped parsley and cilantro.
    • Beat the eggs and pour them into the chicken mixture. Stir gently until the eggs are cooked and the mixture is thick. Remove from heat and allow it to cool.
  2. Prepare the Warqa Dough:
    • If you're using warqa dough, layer about 3-4 sheets, brushing each with melted butter. If using phyllo dough, use 6-7 sheets, layering and buttering each one.
  3. Assemble the Pastilla:
    • Place a layer of the prepared dough in a round pie dish or tagine, ensuring it covers the bottom and sides.
    • Spoon the chicken filling into the center of the dough, spreading it evenly.
    • Fold the dough over the filling to cover it, then add another layer of buttered dough on top, sealing the edges.
    • Carefully flip the pastilla over to ensure the dough is evenly distributed.
  4. Bake the Pastilla:
    • Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
    • Brush the top of the pastilla with melted butter and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Garnish and Serve:
    • Once the pastilla is done, remove it from the oven.
    • Sprinkle powdered sugar and cinnamon on top for a sweet finish.
    • Slice and serve hot.
